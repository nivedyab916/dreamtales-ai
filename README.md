# 🌙 DreamTales AI – Personalized Bedtime Storyteller

DreamTales AI is an AI-powered web application that creates personalized bedtime stories for children. Users can enter details such as the child's name, age, favorite character, moral lesson, story style, and preferred story length. The application then generates a unique, age-appropriate bedtime story with a calming ending.

## 📖 How It Works

1. Enter the child's name.
2. Select the child's age.
3. Choose a favorite character.
4. Enter a moral lesson or theme.
5. Select a story style.
6. Choose the desired story length.
7. Click **Create My Bedtime Story**.
8. Receive a unique AI-generated bedtime story.

## 🎯 Project Objective

The goal of DreamTales AI is to make bedtime more engaging and educational by generating customized stories that encourage imagination, learning, and positive values.
